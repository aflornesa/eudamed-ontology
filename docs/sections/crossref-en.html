SWRL rules

  • Inferència d'incident seriós
  • Monitoratge actiu

Classificació de risc augmentatback to ToC or SWRL ToC



Medical Device c(?d) , is implantable dp(?d,"true"^^boolean) , is active dp(?d,"true"^^boolean) -> has risk class op(?d,?ClassIII_instance)

Inferència d'incident seriósback to ToC or SWRL ToC



Risk c(?r) , has severity dp(?r,"Critical") , involves risk op(?i,?r) -> Serious Incident c(?i)

Monitoratge actiuback to ToC or SWRL ToC



Incident c(?i) , triggers corrective action op(?i,?fsca) , has FSCA status dp(?fsca,"Open") , is reported for op(?i,?d) -> has regulatory framework dp(?d,"UnderMonitoring")

c: Classes
op: Object Properties
dp: Data Properties
